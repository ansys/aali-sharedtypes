=====================
Function Definitions
=====================

.. grid:: 1
   :gutter: 2

   .. grid-item-card:: Structs for Registered Functions
      :class-card: sd-shadow-sm sd-rounded-md
      :text-align: left

      These structs describe available functions and their signatures:

      - `FunctionDefinition`
      - `FunctionInput`
      - `FunctionOutput`
      - `FunctionMetadata`

      They are registered with Flowkit and referenced across LLM and Agent components.
